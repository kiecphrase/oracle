Wilting Sunflowers


Overview

There was never a time the world seemed to be happy. Sure, there was little war, most had food every night, but the whole of the world toiled away in the mundane, never really looking past their own nose. But the winds of change have started rustling the leaves of the trees society has built on, and as the sun sets in a small town nestled gently in a valley between two ancient forests, the winds of change may just have started rustling a tad bit harder...
