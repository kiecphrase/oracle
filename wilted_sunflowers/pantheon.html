Aika

Aliases: The Goddess of Time, Knowledge, The Allseer

One of the 6 Ancients

Constantly searching through time, wandering aimlessly through the past, present, and future. She doesn't seem to care much for the day to day goings-on of the world, but probably because she has seen the thousand outcomes it could have.

Decription: Halfling, Very small, old woman. braided long grey hair, dressed very plainly in a simple natural fiber dress, does not have eyes.

Goddess of : time, seekers of knowledge, libraries, education, schoalrs, learning

Maa

Aliases: Goddess of the Earth

One of the 6 Ancients

Maa has been in a deep slumber for 10,000 years. She, Aalto, and Taivas would constantly clash together to try and gain power over the dominion, but eventually were subdued by Myrsky who cast a spell to make them sleep until their hearts quieted and their rage subdued

Description: Dwarf, strong looking, dark brown skin, coal-black eyes, and green flowing hair, dressed in gold chain-mail

Goddess of : the Earth, Mountains, Dwarves and Dwarvish Races

Aalto

Aliases: God of the Seas, The Great Tortle

One of the 6 Ancients

Aalto has been in a deep slumber for 10,000 years. He, Maa, and Taivas would constantly clash together to try and gain power over the dominion, but eventually were subdued by Myrsky who cast a spell to make them sleep until their hearts quieted and their rage subdued.

Description: Tortle, Dark Blue Shell with Red Swirls, glowing green eyes, dressed in gold chain-mail

God of: The Seas, Sailors, Creatures of the Sea, Tortles

Taivas

Aliases: God of the Sky, Holder of Stars

One of the 6 Ancients

Taivas has been in a deep slumber for 10,000 years. He, Maa, and Aalto would constantly clash together to try and gain power over the dominion, but eventually were subdued by Myrsky who cast a spell to make them sleep until their hearts quieted and their rage subdued.

Description: Aligrine, Light blue / cream colord wings, very light skin, light blue eyes, dressed in gold chain-mail

Myrsky

Aliases: Goddess of Storms, Goddess of Rain, The Crying Ancient, Tsunami Bringer, First Mother

One of the 6 Ancients

Myrsky was the goddess who put Aalto, Maa, and Taivas to sleep to protect the life of the domain from their fighting. She is deeply concerned with the wellbeing of all living things. Extremely tempermental, Myrsky has been known to bring terrible stroms to those who fall out of her favor and good farming weather for those who apease her. Mrsky is also known to be the creator of life in this Dominion along with Kuolema

Description: Elf, Stark White hair and pale white eyes. Dressed in long flowing robes of indeterminate color

Goddess of: Storms, Life, Love, Rage, Farmers

Kuolema

Aliases: God of the Underworld, Death, the Judge, First Father

One of the 6 Ancients

Kuolema has a deep love living things, and presides over them as lord of the underworld. The underworld is a creation of Kuolema's and seperates those who die according to their lives. Kuolema is also known to be the creator of life in this Dominion along with Myrsky

Description: Human, Golden Blonde hair in a set of three braids, tan skin, dark brown eyes, Dressed in a tunic of deep green

God of: Death, The afterline, Love, Justice

Aurinko

Aliases: God of the Sun, the Bright Star, Sundancer

Younger Brother of Kuu

Aurinko and Kuu are the eldest of the Gods after the 6 Ancients, and keep the sun and moon in order. Aurinko looks down proudly over the Earth, marching at a steady pace to keep the day

Description: Halfling, tan skin, bright golden hair, shimmering gold eyes, dressed in a golden tunic

God of the Sun, wheat

Kuu

Aliases: God of the Moon, the Calm Eye

Elder Brother of Aurinko

Aurinko and Kuu are the eldest of the Gods after the 6 Ancients, and keep the sun and moon in order. Kuu watches over the Earth at night, gazing on those that wander and shooing away bad dreams. Kuu every now and then wanders off to the mountains for a night to whittle a new walking stick, but always returns the next night

Description: Halfling, pale skin, grey hair, soft green eyes, dressed in a dark colored tunic, always carrying a walking stick of some type of wood

God of the Moon, dreams, wood carving

Seppa

Aliases: God of the Forge, The Master Smith, the Iron Goddess

Younger Sister of Kaivos

Seppa is the smith of the gods. Seppa spends most of her days in her forge deep below the Earth, crafting metalworks for the gods and greater spirits of the dominion. Seppa gets the ore for her craft from her brother Kaivos. Seppa is blind from when Kepkunema the deep dwarf collapsed Seppa's mine, causing dragon ore fragments to pierce her eyes

Description: Half-Dwarf, soot-covered skin, dirty long hair flowing from under an iron half-healm without eyeholes, dressed in leather wrappings and a leather apron

Goddess of the Forge, metalworkers, craftmen, the blind

Lunastus

Aliases: Goddess of Redemption, Pitty

Twin Sister of Synti

Lunastus and Synti meet the dead at the foot of their father's throne when they first enter the underworld. Lunastus holds the good deads of the dead and makes their case to her father, Kuolema, before final judgement is made

Description: Human, pale skin, brown hair, brown eyes, dressed in a red tunic

Goddess of kind deeds, gentle words, and good thoughts

Metsa

Aliases: God of the Forests, Musiikki

The youngest god

Metsa is the god of the forests. He dances through the thickest wood and densest shrubbs to the sound of his flute, bringing joy and merriment as he goes. Metsa will not hesitate to help out a lost traveler or injured animal, and fiercly defends the wildlands of the Earth. Those who have been helped by metsa recall the distinct smell of rain and Queen Anne's Lace and the impressive flute that Metsa plays, which is at least three times as large as a normal flute and able to produce a dizzying range of tones and pitches

Description: Anthousai, braided pale green hair, deep blue eyes that seem to twinkle, dressed in what looks to be a tunic made of vines and bark, always carring an impressively large and complicated bone flute

God of the forests, beasts, wild things, music

Tulisija

Aliases: God of the Hearth, the Master Cook, The First Friend

The god of men

No one knows when Tulisija first appeard, but the people of the ancient city of Dren noticed the children giggling and playing by the hearth-fires in the dead of winter. Tulisija made himself known to the people of Dren one pirticularly horrible winter when their grain went bad, teaching them to cook the grains into bread, helping them survive the winter

Description: Gnome, very portly, brown shoulder length hair, rosey cheeks, dressed in various furs, carrying a sack of flour and a string of smoked meats

God of the hearth, homes, cooking, baking, cooks, bakers, friendship, community

Kaivos

Aliases: God of the Mines, The Greedy Serpent

Older brother of Seppa

Kaivos is the older brother of Seppa. Kaivos was charged with watching over Seppa, but one day while she mined, Kaivos discoverd the Diamond of Maa, and overtaken by greed, left Seppa to try and take the diamond. When Seppa's mine caved in, Kaivos rushed to her, but was too late to keep her safe. Now, Kaivos digs through mines to get the ore for his sister's work, killing all mortals who get too close to Seppa's forge and any mortals who Kaivos deems too greedy from what they take from the Earth

Description: Half-Dwarf, very dark skin, sunken cheeks, blood-shot eyes, jet black short hair, dressed in a raggity blue tunic

God of the Mines, miners, siblings

Synti

Aliases: Goddess of Punishment, Justice

Twin Sister of Lunastus

Lunastus and Synti meet the dead at the foot of their father's throne when they first enter the underworld. Synti holds the sins of the dead and presents them to her father, Kuolema, before final judgement is made

Description: Human, pale skin, silver hair, brown eyes, dressed in a red tunic

Goddess of the Punishment, justice

Sota

Aliases: God of War, The Glaive-Weilder

younger brother of Tulivouri

Sota was always agressive as a child; always trying to fight something to gain more prestige. Seppa, mad that her work was being used to hunt peaceful beasts, tried to take away his glaive, but Sota learned of the plot, and broke Seppa's hammer before he ran. Now shunned by the gods, Sota enrages the kingdoms with falsehoods to have more opponents to fight

Description: Half-Gnome, fully clad in dented and blood-rusted plate-mail, always holding a large and ornate glaive and square shield

God of war, soilders, gladiators, glory

Tulivouri

Aliases: Goddess of Volcanoes, Goddess of Fire

older sister of Sota

Tulivouri was always known to have a terrible temper, like her brother Sota. Her grandmother, Maa, decided to watch over Tulivouri, but when Maa was put to sleep by Myrsky, Tulivouri's rage and anguish boiled over and destroyed a hundred towns with the lava flow

Description: Half-Gnome, pale skin, red hair, red-tinted eyes, fully clad in a tunic of fire

Goddess of Volcanoes, fire
